Welcome! This application is based on Deep Learning and Flask. It helps you learn music genres easily by analyzing audio files whose genre you might not know.


How to use Music Genre Classifier?


  1. Upload the song you want to classify.
  2. Make sure the audio file ends with .wav or .mp3.
  3. Click the Learn button to get the genre prediction.
